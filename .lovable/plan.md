
# Donald Trust — Nonprofit Website

## Overview
A grand, patriotic website for "Donald Trust," a nonprofit dedicated to protecting and stewarding the @realDonaldTrump username on Manifold Markets for the benefit of humanity's prediction markets ecosystem.

## Pages & Sections

### Landing Page (Single-page scrolling)

1. **Hero Section**
   - Bold headline: "Protecting Truth in Prediction Markets"
   - Subtitle about the mission of Donald Trust
   - Red/white/blue patriotic color scheme with subtle gradient backgrounds
   - Large shield/trust icon or emblem
   - CTA: "Read Our Charter"

2. **Mission Section**
   - Grand statement about why prediction markets matter for humanity
   - Why protecting the @realDonaldTrump handle on Manifold Markets serves the public good (accurate predictions, preventing impersonation, maintaining market integrity)

3. **Charter Section**
   - Formal charter document with articles:
     - Article I: Name & Purpose
     - Article II: Mission — Stewarding the handle for prediction market integrity
     - Article III: Governance — How decisions about the handle are made
     - Article IV: Prohibited Uses — No impersonation, misinformation, market manipulation
     - Article V: Succession & Transfer Terms

4. **Goals Section**
   - Cards/blocks highlighting key goals:
     - Preserve prediction market integrity
     - Prevent impersonation and fraud
     - Ensure responsible stewardship
     - Advance humanity through better forecasting

5. **Handle Usage & Transfer Terms**
   - Clear terms for how @realDonaldTrump will be used
   - Conditions under which the handle could be transferred (e.g., to the actual person, to another qualified steward)
   - Restrictions and safeguards

6. **Footer**
   - "Established 2025" badge
   - Links to charter sections
   - Inspirational closing quote

## Design
- **Colors**: Deep navy (#1a2744), patriotic red (#c41e3a), white, gold accents
- **Typography**: Bold serif headings (Georgia/Playfair), clean sans-serif body
- **Style**: Grand, authoritative — inspired by presidential libraries and constitutional documents
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-ins on scroll, slight parallax on hero
- **Decorative**: Stars, subtle stripe patterns, shield/eagle motifs via CSS/SVG
